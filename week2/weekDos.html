Computer Science

Computer science is the study of the theory, experimentation, and engineering that form the basis for the design and use of computers. It is the scientific and practical approach to computation and its applications and the systematic study of the feasibility, structure, expression, and mechanization of the methodical procedures (or algorithms) that underlie the acquisition, representation, processing, storage, communication of, and access to, information. An alternate, more succinct definition of computer science is the study of automating algorithmic processes that scale. A computer scientist specializes in the theory of computation and the design of computational systems.

In my opinion

To be a computer science master there are 6 fundemental characteristics

Passion

Computer science to my opinion is art. As a developer you have the power to created your own world; show the people what is in your thoughts through code. I concider passion to be important because having this ability to show this art, is not easy -It requires work... But the passion is what motivates you push throught the though times and continue

Persistency

As you continue developing and creating stuff from your computer, there will be many issues and uncertainties. They will drive you crazy, but the key is to keep trying and keep trying. In other words be persistance.

Commitment

As you are creating the genius idea in your head... you will start to discover that it is not turning how you spected it to turn. It might look so different that you will consider deleting everything and restarting... or even quitting. WELL DON'T. You have already commited -Continue that code until you understand what went wrong! By quitting all the time invested will be lost since you didnt truly learn were you went wrong... thus commitment in important

Creativity

Coding is like writting literature. Everyone has their own style of writting and there are more than one solution to every problem. So thinking outside the box, and be creative on how you want to writting to do.

Eagerness to learn

technology is advancing so fast; every day there are newer programming languages and more complex algorithms. Surviving in this industry requires you to enjoy learning and updating to the changes in technology.

Cold Beer

Well... this is self explonatory. After a stressfull time coding, a nice beer always helps relax the brain.
